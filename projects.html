Department of Mathematics and Computing Website

Developing the "Department of Mathematics and Computing" website was a valuable experience in creating a professional and informative platform. The design emphasizes simplicity and clarity, using clean layouts and intuitive navigation to ensure users can easily access program details. The use of side navigation buttons and well-defined content sections ensures a logical flow, while the consistent color scheme reinforces the department's branding. This project taught me the importance of aligning content structure with user needs and maintaining a professional aesthetic for academic contexts.
